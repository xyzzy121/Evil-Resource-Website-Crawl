Explosive A (Resident Evil 3 Remake)

Combine with another explosive A to create explosive rounds, or combine with explosive B to make flame rounds.

A small container of explosive used to create ammunition.

You can use this in the following combinations:
  • Combine with another Explosive A to create Explosive Rounds.
  • Combine with a Explosive B to create Flame Rounds.
