Flame Rounds (Resident Evil 3 Remake)

Ammo for a grenade launcher. The napalm inside ignites on impact, setting the surrounding area on fire.

A canister of shells filled with napalm.

These are used with the MGL Grenade Launcher.
CategoryWeaponry (Ammo)
