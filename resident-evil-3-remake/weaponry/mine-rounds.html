Mine Rounds (Resident Evil 3 Remake)

Ammo for the grenade launcher. Flies to its target using its own propulsion system. Detonates after a short period of time or if approached.

A canister of mined shells that detonate on close proximity or after an extended period of time once fired.

These are used with the MGL Grenade Launcher.
