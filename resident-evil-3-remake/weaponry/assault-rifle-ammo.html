Assault Rifle Ammo (Resident Evil 3 Remake)

5.56mm assault rifle bullets. These low-caliber high-speed rounds are used by various armed forces, especially those under NATO.

A box of assault rifle bullets.

These are used with the CQBR Assault Rifle.
