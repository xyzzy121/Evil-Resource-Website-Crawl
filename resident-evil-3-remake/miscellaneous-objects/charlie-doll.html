Charlie Doll (Resident Evil 3 Remake)

A small porcelain figurine of Charlie McBergen, founder of the Toy Uncle toy company.

These dolls are scattered throughout the game. The aim is to shatter them with a well-aimed bullet or knife strike. Once one has been destroyed that instance of it is permanently removed from the location. One of the game's achievements is to find and destroy all of these.
