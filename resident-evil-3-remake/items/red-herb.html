Red Herb (Resident Evil 3 Remake)

A red herb native to the Arklay Mountains. It can't be used as is.

A herb with medicinal properties.

This cannot be used on its own, but can be combined with a Green Herb to boost its effects, creating the Mixed Herb (G+R).
CategoryItem (Recovery item)
