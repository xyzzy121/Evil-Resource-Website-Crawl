Old Key (Resident Evil 4)

It opens the gate at the end of the back route from the cabin.

A strange shaped odd looking key. The material of which looks to be made from bone.

If you chose to take the right-hand path after surviving the siege of the abandoned house, you need to use this key to unlock the door leading to the gondola area from the gorge.
