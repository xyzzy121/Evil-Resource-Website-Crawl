Assault Rifle for Special Tactics (Resident Evil 6)

This automatic rifle is a powerful weapon with a high rate of fire. Use it to attack groups of enemies at medium and short ranges.

This is Chris' most useful weapon and will be his go-to attack option throughout his campaign. Given its flexibility, prioritise this weapon's ammo over Chris' other weapons. It uses 5.56mm NATO Ammo.

This is one of Chris' default weapons.
