Grenade Rounds (Resident Evil 3: Nemesis)

Grenade Burst Rounds. Standard bullets that scatter fragments when they hit the target. Used for Hk-p Grenade Launcher.

The standard ammunition for the Hk-p Grenade Launcher.

They are highly explosive but the damage isn't as concentrated as the other types of rounds. These can be created by combining various combinations of Gun Powder C's with the Reloading Tool.
