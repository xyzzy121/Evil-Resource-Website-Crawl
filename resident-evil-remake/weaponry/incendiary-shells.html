Incendiary Shells (Resident Evil Remake)

Shells for the Grenade Launcher. They are filled with special fuel that ignites upon impact with target.

Heavy shells filled with a deadly combustive compound, effective for destroying many flammable creatures.

These rounds are used with the Grenade Launcher, they're effective against plant-based enemies such as Plant 42.
