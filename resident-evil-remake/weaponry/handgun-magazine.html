Handgun Magazine (Resident Evil Remake)

A clip for the handgun. 15 rounds of 9mm Parabellum.

A clip of 9mm parabellum rounds for handguns.

These magazines provide an extra fifteen rounds for the following weapons:
  • Handgun
  • Samurai Edge
