Brad Vickers (Resident Evil Remake)

Brad is a computer expert and is an excellent information gatherer. Unfortunately, his fear of dying and lack of enthusiasm for rushing into danger have earned him the nickname "Chickenheart".

Considering you never see his face during the game, there's not a lot to tell about this character. He is the Alpha's pilot who stranded them in the forest when things started going wrong. However, he does retain his credibility at the end when the team need him the most.
