Minimi (Resident Evil)

A full-automatic light-weight machine gun.

A weapon exclusive to Chris in the PC version of the game.

It is quite a powerful weapon and comes loaded with infinite ammunition. It is acquired by completing the game in under three hours, and will appear in your inventory when starting a new game.

Please note: There is some debate over the criteria needed to acquire this weapon, and there are reports that this weapon is only available to certain editions of the PC version.
