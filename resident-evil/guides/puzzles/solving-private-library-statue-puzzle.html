Solving the private library statue puzzle (Resident Evil)

After exploring the guardhouse you will come back to the mansion and explore several new rooms.

If you venture into the private library, you will notice that there is a random statue between the bookshelves that can be pushed and moved around. There is also a switch on the western wall of the room that activates a spotlight, which when activated will be directed at a spot in the north west corner of the room.

Beyond that there no further clues as to what these two seemingly unrelated features correlate to.

How to solve

In actual fact there is nothing particularly cryptic about this puzzle.

There is a statue that can be moved around by your character and a light pointing at a specific spot in the room. If you push the statue into the spot where the light is pointing to, then it will trigger a reveal of a secret area of the library where you can acquire an important item.
