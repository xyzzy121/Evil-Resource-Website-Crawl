Typewriter (Resident Evil 2 Remake)

An ordinary typewriter.

These typewriters allow you to save your current progress in the game. On Easy and Normal difficulties you can use them as many times as you like, but on Hardcore mode you need to use an Ink Ribbon in order to save your progress.
