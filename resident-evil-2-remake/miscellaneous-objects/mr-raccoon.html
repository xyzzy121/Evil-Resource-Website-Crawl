Mr. Raccoon (Resident Evil 2 Remake)

A bobble-head trophy toy depicting the Mr. Raccoon mascot for the local zoo.

There are many of these scattered throughout the game, hidden behind objects or sometimes in plain site. Discovering and shooting them all is one of the fun challenges of the game.
