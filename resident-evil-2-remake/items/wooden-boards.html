Wooden Boards (Resident Evil 2 Remake)

Sturdy pieces of wood that can be used to board up windows. Perfect for keeping the riffraff out.

These can be used to board up windows in the following locations throughout the Police Station. They will prevent any further zombies from crawling through.

  • The window in the south east corner of the East Hallway.
  • The windows on the west side of the South West Hallway.
  • The windows on the west side of the North West Hallway.
  • The window next to the foot of the stairs in the West Stairway 1F.
  • The windows on the east side of the Break Room Hallway.
  • The window in the south east corner of the East Office.
