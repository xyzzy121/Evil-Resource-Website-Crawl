Green Herb (Resident Evil 2 Remake)

A green herb native to the Arklay Mountains. It will restore a small amount of health.

A green-coloured herb that is native to the area.

Using this will restore a small amount of your overall health. It can also be combined with other herbs to enhance overall healing qualities.

The following compounds can be created using Green Herbs and other types of herb:
  • Mixed Herb (G+G)
  • Mixed Herb (G+R)
  • Mixed Herb (G+B)
  • Mixed Herb (G+G+G)
  • Mixed Herb (G+G+B)
  • Mixed Herb (G+R+B)
