High-Grade Gunpowder (Yellow) (Resident Evil 2 Remake)

Combine with gunpowder to make shotgun shells, or combine 2 of these to make MAG ammo.

A small plastic container of a variety of high-grade gunpowder. It can be used to create ammunition. This type of gunpowder is exclusive to Leon.

Use this with the following items to produce the respective results:
  • Combine with Gunpowder to create Shotgun Shells.
  • Combine with Gunpowder (Large) to create a larger amount of Shotgun Shells.
  • Combine with another High-Grade Gunpowder (Yellow) to create MAG Ammo.
