Hand Gun Bullets (Resident Evil 2)

9x19 parabellum rounds that can be used for either the H&K VP70 or Browning HP.

A box of fifteen 9mm parabellum rounds.

These bullets are used with the following weapons:
  • H&K VP70
  • H&K VP70 Burst
  • Browning HP
  • Colt S.A.A.
