Save point (Resident Evil Gaiden)

The save point is an opportunity to save your progress. These appear automatically throughout your adventure when you reach certain points in the story, and you cannot save your progress outside of these occurrences. There are three slots available, which when chosen will overwrite the save that was saved there previously.
CategoryMiscellaneous object
