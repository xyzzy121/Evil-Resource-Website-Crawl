Leonardo's House - Village (Resident Evil Village)

The house belonging to a man named Leonardo.

The east door of this house is locked from the inside. You need to access the house via the south west door and unlock it.

This place is linked to the following locations: Leonardo's Back Yard, Leonardo's Front Yard, Maiden of War.
