Poultry (Resident Evil Village)

Meat gathered from birds of this region. Common ingredient. Cannot be eaten as is.

This is one of several types of ingredient you can find that you can either sell to The Duke for Lei, or instead provide to him to cook into delicious meals. These meals will provide you with various stat boosts, such as health increase, speed increase and reducing the amount of damage you receive from enemies.

This particular ingredient can be used in the following recipes:
  • Herbed Fish
  • Bird and Beast Pilaf
  • Three-Flavored Mititei
  • Tochitura de Pui
CategoryCooking (Ingredient)
