Lockpick (Resident Evil Village)

A tool for opening simple locks. Each one can only be used once.

A device for opening simple locks.

There are several available in the game, and each one can only be used once. They can be used in the following locations:
  • Unlocks the draw in the Living Room of Castle Dimitrescu.
  • Unlocks the draw in the Eastern Corridor of Castle Dimitrescu.
  • Unlocks the draw in the Opera Hall of Castle Dimitrescu.
  • Unlocks the draw in the back bedroom of the Chapel in the village.
  • Unlocks the draw in the West Old Town Farmer's House in the village.
  • Unlocks the draw in Leonardo's House in the village.
  • Unlocks the fridge in the Generator Room Walkway of Heisenberg's Factory.
  • Unlocks the draw in the Private Passageway area of Heisenberg's Factory.
