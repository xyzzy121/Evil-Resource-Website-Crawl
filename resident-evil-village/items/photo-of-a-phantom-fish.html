Photo of a Phantom Fish (Resident Evil Village)

A photo of a dazzling golden fish. Where was it taken?

A photograph showing a brilliant golden fish swimming in a pond.

This is one of several photographs you can acquire that shows the location of a rare animal that you can hunt and acquire food items from in order to either sell or create cooking recipes with. This specific photo points you in the direction of a rare golden salmon that takes up residence in the water pool in the village's southern riverbank. Killing it will yield you a reward of a piece of Finest Fish.
CategoryItem (Key item)

Location

  • Dungeon - Stronghold B1

    On the small trolley in the south west cell.
