Metal Scrap (Resident Evil Village)

A small pile of various metal scrap parts. This is a crafting item that cannot be used on its own, but instead combined with something else to produce a new item.

You can use this in the following combinations:
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Gunpowder to create 1 Pipe Bomb (once you have the Pipe Bombs recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Rusted Scrap to create 1 Mine (once you have the Mines recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Gunpowder and 1 Herb to create 2 Explosive Rounds (once you have the Explosive Rounds recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 4 Gunpowder and 4 Rusted Scrap to create 3 Magnum Ammo (once you have the Magnum Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Rusted Scrap and 1 Chem Fluid to create 20 Rifle Ammo (once you have the Rifle Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 2 Herbs and 2 Rusted Scrap to create 5 Rocket Pistol Ammo (once you have the Rocket Pistol Ammo recipe).
