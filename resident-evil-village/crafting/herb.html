Herb (Resident Evil Village)

A green herb that is common to this specific region, thought to contain medicinal properties. This is a crafting item that cannot be used on its own, but instead combined with something else to produce a new item.

You can use this in the following combinations:
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Chem Fluid to create a First Aid Med.
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Gunpowder and 1 Metal Scrap to create 2 Explosive Rounds (once you have the Explosive Rounds recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Chem Fluid and 1 Rusted Scrap to create 2 Flashbangs (once you have the Flashbangs recipe).
  • Combine 2 of these with 2 Rusted Scrap and 1 Metal Scrap to create 5 Rocket Pistol Ammo (once you have the Rocket Pistol Ammo recipe).
