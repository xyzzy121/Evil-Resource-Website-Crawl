Chem Fluid (Resident Evil Village)

A bottle of fluid that appears to be some sort of chemical with medicinal properties. This is a crafting item that cannot be used on its own, but instead combined with something else to produce a new item.

You can use this in the following combinations:
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Herb to create a First Aid Med.
  • Combine 1 of these with 2 Rusted Scrap and 1 Gunpowder to create 3 Shotgun Ammo (once you have the Shotgun Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 2 Rusted Scrap and 2 Gunpowder and 1 Rusted Scrap to create 4 Sniper Rifle Ammo (once you have the Sniper Rifle Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Rusted Scrap and 1 Herb to create 2 Flashbangs (once you have the Flashbangs recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Rusted Scrap and 1 Metal Scrap to create 20 Rifle Ammo (once you have the Rifle Ammo recipe).
