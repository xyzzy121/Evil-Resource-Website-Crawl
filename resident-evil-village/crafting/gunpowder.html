Gunpowder (Resident Evil Village)

A tin of gunpowder. This is a crafting item that cannot be used on its own, but instead combined with something else to produce a new item.

You can use this in the following combinations:
  • Combine 2 of these with 2 Rusted Scrap to create 15 Handgun Ammo.
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Metal Scrap to create 1 Pipe Bomb (once you have the Pipe Bombs recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 2 Rusted Scrap and 1 Chem Fluid to create 3 Shotgun Ammo (once you have the Shotgun Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 2 of these with 1 Rusted Scrap and 1 Chem Fluid to create 4 Sniper Rifle Ammo (once you have the Sniper Rifle Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Metal Scrap and 1 Herb to create 2 Explosive Rounds (once you have the Explosive Rounds recipe).
  • Combine 4 of these with 4 Rusted Scrap and 1 Metal Scrap to create 3 Magnum Ammo (once you have the Magnum Ammo recipe).
