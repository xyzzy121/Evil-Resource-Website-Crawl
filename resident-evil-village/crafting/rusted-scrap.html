Rusted Scrap (Resident Evil Village)

A small pile of various rusted scrap parts. This is a crafting item that cannot be used on its own, but instead combined with something else to produce a new item.

You can use this in the following combinations:
  • Combine 2 of these with 2 Gunpowder to create 15 Handgun Ammo.
  • Combine 2 of these with 1 Gunpowder and 1 Chem Fluid to create 3 Shotgun Ammo (once you have the Shotgun Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 2 Gunpowder and 1 Chem Fluid to create 4 Sniper Rifle Ammo (once you have the Sniper Rifle Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Metal Scrap to create 1 Mine (once you have the Mines recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Chem Fluid and 1 Herb to create 2 Flashbangs (once you have the Flashbangs recipe).
  • Combine 4 of these with 4 Gunpowder and 1 Metal Scrap to create 3 Magnum Ammo (once you have the Magnum Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 1 of these with 1 Metal Scrap and 1 Chem Fluid to create 20 Rifle Ammo (once you have the Rifle Ammo recipe).
  • Combine 2 of these with 2 Herbs and 1 Metal Scrap to create 5 Rocket Pistol Ammo (once you have the Rocket Pistol Ammo recipe).
