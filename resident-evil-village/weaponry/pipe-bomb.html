Pipe Bomb (Resident Evil Village)

A handmade pipe bomb. When thrown, it explodes violently upon impact or after a set amount of time.

A weapon you can throw that will detonate and cause massive damage to nearby enemies.

These explosives also have a practical use. You can use them to destroy cracked walls throughout various locations to access additional areas. These locations are listed as follows:
  • Use one on the cracked wall behind the movable shelves inside Castle Dimitrescu's Armory in order to defeat Cassandra Dimitrescu.
  • Use one on the cracked wall inside Castle Dimitrescu's Special Chambers to solve the puzzle in this room.
  • Use one on the cracked wall in the south west corner of Heisenberg's Factory's Backup Generator Room in order to access a secret room with additional items.
  • Use one on the cracked wall on the north side of Heisenberg's Factory's MB3 Maintenance Passage to access a hidden room with lots of valuables inside.
