Magnum Ammo (Resident Evil Village)

Fifty-caliber magnum cartridges. Packed with powder for a powerful shot that can pierce most armor.

Bullets designed for magnum handguns.

These rounds are used with the following weapons:
  • M1851 Wolfsbane
  • S.T.A.K.E.
CategoryWeaponry (Ammunition)
Sell price700 Lei
