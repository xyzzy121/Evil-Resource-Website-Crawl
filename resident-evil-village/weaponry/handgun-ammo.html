Handgun Ammo (Resident Evil Village)

9mm parabellum rounds for a handgun. Generic rounds that have little stopping power, but are easy to handle.

Standard calibre bullets compatible with most handguns.

These rounds are used with the following weapons:
  • LEMI
  • M1911
  • V61 Custom
  • USM-AI
  • Samurai Edge - AW Model-01
