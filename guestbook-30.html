  • Alex
    about 8 years ago

    Hi Raven. Well, it's not that I can't add new games, as you have seen I recently added Survivor to the site. In fact I'm currently working on the Resident Evil Outbreak games.

    But it's a fairly long process to add a new game, what with having to get all the details for the all items, characters, enemies, files etc, getting all of their locations, creating all of their images, writing up a description for each. Then creating the clickable maps with all the room names. Uploading all of this information into the database and binding them all together. To do all this I play the games myself to ensure the accuracy of the data, so I first need to complete each game several times before I'm finished collecting all that I need. Plus I need to find the time to do all this around my job and my social life, whilst not neglecting my partner.

    So overall it's not a quick process :-)
