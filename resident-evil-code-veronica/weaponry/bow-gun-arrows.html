Bow Gun Arrows (Resident Evil Code: Veronica)

An iron-made arrow for use with a bowgun.

A pack of iron bow gun bolts for the Cross Bow Handgun.

They can either be used with the bow gun, or combined with the Bow Gun Powder to create Gun Powder Arrows.
