Message from management (Resident Evil Outbreak File #2)

This file is available during the "Wild Things" scenario.

It contains the passcode needed to restore power to the Zoo. Once you have the passcode it can be entered into the terminal inside the Inner office.
CategoryFile (Wild Things)

Transcript

"Message from management"

Lloyd:
The new front gate locking mechanism is kind of a pain. Ever since the incident, the folks that live around here have been clamoring for tighter security. We have to listen to them.

Anyway, first you've got to check the office to make sure the main power is on. Then, you need to put the emblems in the 2 lion statues. That activates the lock. Be sure to store the emblems separately.

Don't forget the password for the electrical system, too. It's changed every week, so be on your toes. This week, it's ####. I know it's a pain, but I'm counting on you, Lloyd.

Raccoon Zoo Management
