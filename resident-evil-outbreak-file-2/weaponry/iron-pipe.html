Iron Pipe (Resident Evil Outbreak File #2)

A pipe made of iron.

A heavy iron pipe that can be used as a weapon, capable of causing a small amount of damage.

When used a certain number of times, it will eventually degrade and turn into a Curved Iron Pipe.

David can create two additional weapons using this:
  • He can combine this with a Battery using a piece of Vinyl Tape to create the Stun Rod.
  • He can combine this with a Concrete Piece using a piece of Vinyl Tape to create the Hammer.
