Magnum Revolver (Resident Evil Outbreak File #2)

An M29-44 magnum. It uses 44 magnum rounds.

A six-shot high calibre revolver. When fired, most characters will need a moment to recover from the recoil.

Its ammunition is Magnum Revolver Rounds and it is also compatible with the Magnum Revolver S. Loader.
