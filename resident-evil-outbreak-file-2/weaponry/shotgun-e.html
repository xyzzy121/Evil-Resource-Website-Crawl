Shotgun E (Resident Evil Outbreak File #2)

An easy to handle shotgun. It uses 12 gauge rounds.

A standard pump-action hunting shotgun equipped with an extended barrel and stock for long-range shooting.

Its ammunition is Shotgun Rounds.
