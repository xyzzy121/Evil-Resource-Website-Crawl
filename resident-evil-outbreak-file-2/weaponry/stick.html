Stick (Resident Evil Outbreak File #2)

A short wooden pole.

A small stick of wood. Throwing these at an enemy will inflict a small amount of damage and can cause the enemy to stagger backwards slightly, allowing you to run past.

This is what you get if you use a Wooden Pole one too many times.
