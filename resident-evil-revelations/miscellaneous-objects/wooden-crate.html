Wooden Crate (Resident Evil Revelations)

This is a standard wooden crate that you will find scattered throughout the campaign mode of the game. You need to destroy the crate in order to acquire its contents. Since it only takes one hit from any weapon to break open, it is recommended you use your combat knife to save bullets. The contents of the crate are sometimes consistent, but at other times you will find that the game will throw you a random stash of ammunition or a Green Herb.
