Weapon Box (Resident Evil Revelations)

The Weapon Box is a permanent storage crate for all your weapons and Custom Parts. Every weapon you have acquired in campaign mode will be in here. This crate also serves as the tool for customising your weapons with whatever Custom Parts you have acquired.
