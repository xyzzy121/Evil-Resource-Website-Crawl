Hidden Item (Resident Evil Revelations)

This isn't a specific item, but indicates that an item is available if you scan the area with the Genesis Scanner. The item could end up being hidden cache of ammunition, a Green Herb, or one of the types of Grenade you have available to you. Sometimes the game will remain consistent with what item you acquire, but in most cases the item you get will be random each time you play.
