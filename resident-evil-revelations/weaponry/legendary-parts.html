Legendary Parts (Resident Evil Revelations)

A case containing weapon parts that are sought after by amateurs.

These are blue-coloured cases that contain much more powerful add-ons and enhancements for weapon compared to the standard Custom Parts. These parts are only available on the HD port of the game, and can only be found when playing Infernal mode.
CategoryWeaponry (Weapon part)
