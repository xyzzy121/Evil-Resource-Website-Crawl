Hand Grenade (Resident Evil Revelations)

A destructive hand-thrown explosive. Works well on clusters of enemies.

This grenade is the base explosive weapon and found commonly throughout the game. Grenades inflict a fair amount of damage and are particularly effective against large groups of medium-sized monsters.
