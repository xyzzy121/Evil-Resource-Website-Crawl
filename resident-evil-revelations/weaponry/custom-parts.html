Custom Parts (Resident Evil Revelations)

Case containing parts for firearm customization.

These are green-coloured cases that contain standard add-ons and enhancements that can be applied to your weapons to improve their performance and power.

You can carry a maximum of 10 parts at a given time. You won't know which part you have acquired until you access a Weapon Box.
