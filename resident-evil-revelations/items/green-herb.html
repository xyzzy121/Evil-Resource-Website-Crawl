Green Herb (Resident Evil Revelations)

A ground down medicinal plant. Relieves fatigue and heals wounds.

A herb that has medicinal properties, originally found growing in the Raccoon City area.

A single herb will restore you to full health. You can carry a maximum of five herbs during Campaign mode.
