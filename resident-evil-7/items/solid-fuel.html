Solid Fuel (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A flammable solid that burns fiercely when ignited. Can't be used as is.

A block of flammable material.

This is primarily used for creating highly-combustible weaponry. It cannot be used on its own, but instead combined with something to create the following results:
  • Combine with Chem Fluid to create ×100 Burner Fuel.
  • Combine with Strong Chem Fluid to create ×2 Flame Rounds.
