Herb (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A plant containing nutrients that help to heal wounds. Consume to recover a small amount of health.

A herb that can be found growing naturally in the Louisiana area.

Using this will restore a very small portion of your overall health.
CategoryItem (Consumable)
