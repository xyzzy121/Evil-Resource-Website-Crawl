Lock Pick (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A tool for opening simple locks. Breaks easily.

A picking tool capable of unlocking simple locks. The tool is fragile and can only unlock a single lock before breaking.

In Easy and Normal mode, you can use a lock pick in the following locations:
  • The draw of the blue cabinet in the Guest House's Kitchen. Note: this can only be done during the events of the "Derelict House Footage" video tape.
  • The draw of the cabinet in the south west corner of the Main House's Main Corridor.
  • The box on the table in the Main House's Small Workshop.
  • The desk draw in the Main House's Study.
  • The box on the metal shelves in the Main House's Balcony.
  • The desk draw in the Old House's Dining Room.
  • The draw of the small white unit in the Old House's Potting Room.
  • The toolbox in the Wrecked Ship's Control Room.

In Madhouse mode, you can use the lock pick in the following locations:
  • The draw of the blue cabinet in the Guest House's Kitchen. Note: this can only be done during the events of the "Derelict House Footage" video tape.
  • The toolbox on the workbench in the Main House's Garage.
  • The chest on the table in the Processing Area's Meat Storage.
  • The chest in the south east corner of the Old House's Walkway.
  • The chest on the table in the Old House's Potting Room.
  • The toolbox in the Testing Area's Barn Office.
  • The toolbox in the Boat House's Shed.
  • The toolbox inside the Salt Mine's Cultivation Room.
