Corrosive (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A strong acid that can dissolve certain metals.

A highly corrosive acid in a squeezable bottle. This acid can dissolve metal locks, and can be used in the following locations:

Whilst exploring the ship during the course of the Old Videotape flashback scenario:
  • On the S2 level, use on the door in the Workers Access Passage 2 to access the No. 2 Engine Room.
  • On the S2 level, use on the door in the Straight Corridor to access the Store Room.
  • On the 2F, use on the wall panel in the Bunk Room.
  • On the 2F, use on the wall panel in the north east corner of the Main Corridor.

Whilst exploring the wrecked ship:
  • On the 3F, use it on the door in the Control Room to access the Main Corridor.
  • On the 2F, use it on the door in the Main Corridor to access the southern half of the Bunk Room.
  • On the 1F, use it on the door in the Main Corridor to access the Recreation Room.
  • On the S2 level, use it on the wall panel in the Workers Access Passage 2.
  • On the S2 level, use it on the wall panel in the Cargo Bay.
