Chem Fluid (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A liquid that can be combined to make items. Can't be used as is.

A bag of yellow liquid.

This item cannot be used on its own. However, it can be used in the following combinations of other ingredients to create new items:
  • Combine with a Herb to create a First Aid Med.
  • Combine with Gunpowder to create ×10 Handgun Ammo.
  • Combine with Solid Fuel to create ×100 Burner Fuel.
  • Combine with Supplements to create Psychostimulants.
