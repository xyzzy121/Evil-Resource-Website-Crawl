Walking Shoes (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

Light shoes to make walking more comfortable. Increases walking speed, but running speed stays the same.

An ordinary pair of comfortable walking shoes. These are a special item that are awarded to you for destroying all the Mr Everywhere figurines. You will find this item inside an Item Box after unlocking it.

Having these inside your inventory will increase your walking speed, making it easier to evade and out-walk enemies.
CategoryItem (Special item)
