Strong Chem Fluid (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

Can be combined with other items to change their properties. Stronger than normal Chem Fluid.

A bag of red liquid.

This item cannot be used on its own. However, it can be used in the following combinations of other ingredients to create new items:
  • Combine with a Herb to create a Strong First Aid Med.
  • Combine with Gunpowder to create ×10 Enhanced Handgun Ammo.
  • Combine with Solid Fuel to create ×2 Flame Rounds.
  • Combine with Supplements to create ×2 Neuro Rounds.
