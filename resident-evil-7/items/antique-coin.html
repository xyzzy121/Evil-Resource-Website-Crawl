Antique Coin (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

An old, commemorative coin.

A small bronze coin that appears to be very old.

There are quite a number of these scattered throughout the game. Their use becomes apparent once you discover birdcages. These birdcages will contain useful items inside, but they can only be unlocked by providing each with a certain number of these coins.
