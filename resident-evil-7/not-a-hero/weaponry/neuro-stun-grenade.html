Neuro-stun Grenade (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard - Not a Hero)

A hand grenade that releases a paralyzing gas that stuns nearby enemies.

A special type of the grenade that explodes and emits a compound that will temporarily stun any enemies caught in its blast radius, allowing you to get past unharmed. These types of grenade do work in killing the smaller Little Crawler variants you encounter.
