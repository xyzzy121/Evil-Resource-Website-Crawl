Remote Bomb (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A bomb that can be detonated by remote control.

A bomb that can triggered to explode remotely. It is activated by placing it on the ground and triggering it from a safe distance.

It has a devastating effect on anything caught in the vicinity of its explosion.
