MPM Handgun (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A small, standard power handgun acquired on the wrecked ship.

A handgun that is similar in power to the G17 Handgun, except it only holds 9 rounds.

It can be loaded with either Handgun Ammo or Enhanced Handgun Ammo.
