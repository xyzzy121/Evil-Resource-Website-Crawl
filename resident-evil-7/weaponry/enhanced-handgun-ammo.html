Enhanced Handgun Ammo (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

Ammo packed with extra gunpowder, making it more powerful.

A box of enhanced handgun bullets. These rounds are more powerful and have greater stopping power than the standard Handgun Ammo. These rounds can be used with the following weapons:
  • M19 Handgun
  • G17 Handgun
  • MPM Handgun
  • Albert-01R
