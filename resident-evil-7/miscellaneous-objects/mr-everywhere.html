Mr Everywhere (Resident Evil 7 Biohazard)

A small bobble-head sports figurine.

As the name implies, these little guys are located in lots of different places throughout the game. You know you're in the immediate vicinity of one when you here a soft jingling rattling sound. If you destroy all 20 of these you will receive bonus items after completing the game.
