Handgun for Mark (Resident Evil Outbreak)

Mark's favourite handgun. It uses 9mm parabellum rounds.

The key item for Mark. An automatic pistol that has been customized by Mark. It fires 9mm parabellum rounds.

Its ammunition is Handgun Rounds and it is also compatible with the Handgun Magazine.
