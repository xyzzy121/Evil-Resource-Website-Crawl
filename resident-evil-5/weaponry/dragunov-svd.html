Dragunov SVD (Resident Evil 5)

The first of the semi-auto rifles enables the sniper to take a bead and squeeze off two shots in a row if the first misses its target or a follow-up is required. It also means that creatures with plentiful health and armour can be kept in sight from distance without losing the target. This weapon uses Rifle ammo.
