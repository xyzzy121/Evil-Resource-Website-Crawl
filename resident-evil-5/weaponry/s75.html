S75 (Resident Evil 5)

This is a bolt-action rifle so the sniper will lose their scope sight immediately after firing in order to load another cartridge. The reload speed is irrelevant for the most part as this is a slow weapon anyway. The power of the rifle is immense at such an early stage in the game, so even body shots will be sufficient to take down many ordinary targets. This weapon uses Rifle ammo.

Fully upgrade this weapon to unlock the Longbow.
