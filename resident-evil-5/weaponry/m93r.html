M93R (Resident Evil 5)

Unusually, this weapon has it laser pointer placed over the barrel, and the grip has a folding stock for automatic fire.The special ability cannot be upgraded, but this pistol can fire a 3-round burst. You must hold down the fire button for this effect, so individual shots can still be achieved if you're running low on ammo. This weapon uses Handgun ammo.

This weapon is available for purchase after fully upgrading the M92F.
