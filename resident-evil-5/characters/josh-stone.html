Josh Stone (Resident Evil 5)

A veteran solider and member of the BSAA West African branch, Josh leads the Delta Team tasked with the capture of Ricardo Irving. His deep compassion towards others earns him the trust of the men serving under him. Sheva also trained under him when she first entered the BSAA.
