Bruce McGivern (Resident Evil: Dead Aim)

Bruce is a member of the US Strategic Command Forces (U.S. STRATCOM). His role is primarily working as an engineer on an Anti-Umbrella investigation task force.

Bruce comes across as very reckless, not always thinking things through but instead acting on impulse. This results in a seemingly disregard for his own life, yet he always stays committed to the mission at hand.
