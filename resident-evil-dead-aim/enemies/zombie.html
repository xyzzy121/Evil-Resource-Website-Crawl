Zombie (Resident Evil: Dead Aim)

The most frequently encountered enemy of the game. These creatures are the result of what happens when a human becomes infected by the T-Virus. Their desire for human flesh is what drives these walking corpses, and even though they are slow moving, encountering them in large numbers can prove to be fatal.
