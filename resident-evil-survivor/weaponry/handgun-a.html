Handgun A (Resident Evil Survivor)

Glock 17. This is a small lightweight gun, which is made from plastic and is famous for its stability.

A Glock 17 handgun that uses 9mm parabellum rounds. It can hold seventeen rounds, and is fairly average when it comes to fire power, reload speed and rate of fire.
